About ANWESHA

Visual Artist, Kolkata

About My Art

I am a full-time visual artist based in Kolkata, India, working primarily with acrylic, ink, charcoal, conte, and soft pastels. My practice draws inspiration from human expressions and mythological narratives, blending realism—especially in portraits and figures—with an abstract signature style.

With an academic background in Human Physiology and published contributions in scientific books, I find my scientific understanding influencing the structure and depth of my compositions. This unique perspective allows me to explore stories through art while maintaining technical precision.

Education & Training

  • Diploma in Fine Arts, Sarbo Bharatiya-Charu-O-Karu Kala Niket
  • Academic background in Human Physiology
  • Workshops in tempera, watercolour, cyanotype techniques

Exhibitions & Awards

  • ICCR Kolkata
  • Birla Academy of Art & Culture
  • Academy of Fine Arts
  • Kolkata Center for Creativity (Emami Gallery)
  • Bhaswati Bose Award
  • Bangiya Kala Sampurna Award

Community Engagement

I engage in community-based projects including art therapy workshops for differently-abled children. Several of my artworks are housed in permanent collections, and I continue to develop my skills through various painting technique workshops.
